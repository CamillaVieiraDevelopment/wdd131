São José dos Pinhais - Paraná - Brasil _____________________________ São José dos Pinhais is a city located in southern Brazil, in the state of Paraná, near the capital Curitiba and the region’s main international airport. With strong industrial development, especially in the automotive and logistics sectors. The city combines economic growth with a good quality of life. Paraná, in turn, is known for its cultural diversity, remarkable natural landscapes such as Iguaçu Falls, and for being one of Brazil’s states with the best infrastructure, education, and agricultural production, playing a strategic role in the country.
